Who Am I?

I don’t just dream things—I build them. Everything I create, from visuals to music to code, has my fingerprints on it.

My work isn’t random. I work with intention. I break things down and rebuild them until they feel exactly right.

My aesthetic is strong. Clean layouts. Dark energy. Smooth animations. Heavy drops. Everything I put out reflects my style—if it carries my name, it’s done right.

I might not be the best—but I’m giving it my all, and it shows.

What I Do

I create. From music that resonates with emotion to digital art that brings my imagination to life, I express myself through various mediums.

My passion for 3D art, paired with occasional ventures into 2D, allows me to craft visually striking designs.

Photography is another outlet where I capture and edit moments, telling stories through the lens.

Alongside this, I immerse myself in gaming, constantly seeking new experiences, and am even developing my own game.

This website? It’s my canvas, a space where I break free from convention and shape a digital identity that reflects my evolving creativity. Looking a bit Empty? That's because My Story has just Begun.

Things About Me

I’m Dennis, a 21-year-old from Germany. I create music, digital art, and photography, always pushing myself to learn new things and expand my horizons.

My journey in music is inspired by artists like Rooler, Sickmode, with a style rooted in Hardstyle and Rawstyle, while my softer side is shaped by artists like Burna Boy, Tekno, and Ayra Starr.

In addition to creating music, I’m deeply involved in digital art—especially 3D art.

I’m also passionate about photography, capturing moments, and editing photos to tell visual stories.

Gaming is another one of my passions. I dive into games to experience new worlds, and I’m even working on developing my own game.

This website is my personal space where I can break free from conventions and truly reflect my evolving creativity. If it feels a bit empty, that’s because my story has only just begun.
